2023

Death at the Esplanade - Short Film

Short Film

A short film based and filmed in Ballyvaughan Co.Clare about a failing hotel. The film is currently being entered into multiple film festivals and has been accepted into Achill Island and Ashbourne film festivals. I had numerous roles in the project from early character design sketches to creating brochures and booklets for the screening of the film in the Lighthouse Cinema. My main role was the Graphic Designer creating posters, layovers, etc. I used various applications such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, Lightroom, and After Effects.

Production Designer: From meticulously crafting sets and props to ensuring every detail aligns with the director's vision. I passionately constructed immersive environments in the hopes of captivating the audience.
Prop Design: Over two months, I sourced and handcrafted most of the props from scratch.
Costume Designer: Early in the production process, I sketched multiple proposed character designs and established the color schemes designated for each character. I constructed these out of charity shop finds and minor alterations to clothes that we already had.
Makeup Artist: Did slight touch-ups each morning on a few actors to make them appear slightly older or slightly more youthful.
Social Media Manager: I was in charge of curating posts for the film's Instagram @death_at_the_esplanade featuring a synopsis and film BTS.
Lighting/ Gaffer: On set, I worked with the director and Cinematographer to light the scenes as imagined in the storyboards as well as being the gaffer.
